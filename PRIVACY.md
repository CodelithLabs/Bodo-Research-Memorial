# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

## Overview

Bodo Research Memorial collects minimal data required to operate the platform and protect services.

## Data We May Process

- Account details for authenticated users (name, email, role)
- Submitted content and revision notes
- Technical metadata for security (IP, rate-limit and audit signals)
- Basic analytics/diagnostics as configured by deployment

## Purpose of Processing

- Authenticate and authorize users
- Maintain editorial workflows
- Prevent abuse and unauthorized access
- Preserve quality and integrity of archive data

## Retention

Data is retained as necessary for operation, legal compliance, and auditability.

## Your Rights

Subject to local law, you may request access, correction, or deletion of personal data where applicable.

## Security

Reasonable technical and organizational controls are used to protect data.
